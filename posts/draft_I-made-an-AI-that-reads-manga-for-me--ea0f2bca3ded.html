I made an AI that reads manga for me.

So, I am a weeb. I love anime but I am also a bookworm so naturally I am a very big manga reader. 

I love reading manga. They are the escape from reality, like portals to another worlds, also with amazing visuals you can imagine what worlds the author actually wants you to see.

So, some of the larger manga’s are normally divided into arcs and some arcs can be good some can be bad some are called filler arcs that are not important to the story but are there to make the story longer. And these filler arcs are boring they are so boring that they motivated me to make an AI that can read and summarise filler arcs for me from any manga.

What will be our inputs?

  • Dialogues from every characters
  • The flow of dialogues 
  • visual image

What is our expected output ?

  • A paragraph summarising everything about the arc.
